SpaceSnake
Instructions:

SpaceSnake is a prototype attempt to recreate the classic Gremlin Industries game 'Blockade', originally released in 1976.

  • 1: Use the arrow keys to move the Snake and intercept the 'Food' blocks.
  • 2: Don't let the Snake hit the sides or you will lose.
  • 3: You will score 10 for each block eaten.
  • 4: Use the button to reload.
