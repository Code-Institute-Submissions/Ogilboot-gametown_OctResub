SpaceSnake

SpaceSnake is a prototype attempt to recreate the classic Gremlin Industries game 'Blockade', originally released in 1976.

Instructions:

Use the Arrow keys to move your snake around the game-area and eat the Chunks of food. You will score 1 point for each Chunk eaten! Make sure not to hit the walls or yourself or you will lose the game! Use the 'Reload Game' button to reload.
