BlockBattle
Instructions:

BlockBattle is a prototype attempt to recreate the classic Atari game 'Breakout', originally released on May 13, 1976.

  • 1: Use the Left & Right arrow keys to move the paddle and intercept the bouncing ball into the blocks.
  • 2: Don't let the ball hit the bottom line or you will lose.
  • 3: You will score 1 for each block destroyed.
  • 4: Use the Game button to reload.
